How to Play

🃏 The Deck

20 cards — 6 Aces, 6 Kings, 6 Queens, 2 Jokers. Each player gets 5 cards. Jokers are wild and count as any rank.

🔄 Each Round

A rank is declared (Ace, King, or Queen). Everyone must claim to play that rank — but you can play any card face-down.

🎴 On Your Turn

Select 1–3 cards and play them face-down. You must declare you're playing the round's rank, even if you're bluffing.

🤥 Calling UWONGO

Instead of playing, you can call UWONGO on the previous player. Their cards are revealed. If they were lying, they lose. If they told the truth, YOU lose.

🔫 Losing a Challenge

The loser picks up the entire pile, then pulls the trigger on a Russian Roulette revolver. Progressive odds — 1/6, then 2/6, then 3/6... If it fires — eliminated! If not — survive and play on.

🏆 Winning

Last player (or team) standing wins! Survive the bluffs, survive the bullets.
